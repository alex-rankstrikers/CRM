Decorated in warm, relaxing tones, the popular Heritage Rooms have views across the private gardens or the Royal Crescent Lawns. Perfect for couples looking for a romantic break or those travelling with a young child. Floris bath and body products are standard in all of the en-suite bathrooms which have baths or showers and a baby cot is available on request. Pets allowed in some Heritage rooms (charges apply).

Amenities: garden view, hairdryer, shower, telephone, towels, air conditioning, Wi-Fi/Internet, flat screen TV, safety deposit box, fresh flowers, water, hairdryer, duvet, bathrobe, slippers
